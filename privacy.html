Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

🔒 SilentCut processes all files locally in your browser. Your media files are never uploaded, transmitted, or stored on any server.

Overview

SilentCut is a browser-based tool that removes silence from video and audio files. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we do and do not collect.

Files You Process

When you use SilentCut to process a video or audio file:

  • The file is processed entirely on your device using WebAssembly (FFmpeg.wasm)
  • No file data is transmitted to any server
  • No file data is stored persistently — everything exists only in browser memory
  • When you close the tab, all processed data is discarded

Data We Collect

SilentCut itself does not collect any personal data. We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics services.

However, the site is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may log standard HTTP request data (IP address, user agent, timestamp) as part of their infrastructure. This is governed by GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Third-Party Resources

SilentCut loads the following resources from CDNs on first use:

  • FFmpeg.wasm core (~30 MB) from jsDelivr CDN
  • Google Fonts (Inter typeface)

These CDN providers may have their own privacy policies. After the initial download, FFmpeg assets may be cached by your browser and no further requests are made.

Donations

If you choose to donate, you will be redirected to Polar.sh, a third-party payment platform. Your payment information is handled entirely by Polar.sh and is governed by their privacy policy. SilentCut does not receive or store any payment details.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at hello@silentcut.site.
